## FAQ

Question:
```text
Does LifeThreads upload my photos?
```

Answer:
```text
No. The current version is local-first and does not upload your memories or photos to GK Coding.
```

Question:
```text
Is LifeThreads a social app?
```

Answer:
```text
No. There is no public profile, social feed, or follower system in the current version.
```

Question:
```text
Why not a subscription?
```

Answer:
```text
A private memory app should be easy to trust. The first premium version is planned as a one-time lifetime unlock, not a monthly subscription.
```

Question:
```text
Will iOS come later?
```

Answer:
```text
Yes, iOS is planned after Android validation.
```
